# Simple Furusato Donation Limit Table

Use this only for rough, salary-earner estimates. Prefer current official or municipality sources when accuracy matters.

Sources to verify against when browsing is available:

- Soumu furusato nozei portal, deduction mechanism pages
- NTA Tax Answer No.1155 for the legal outline of donation deductions
- Municipality copies of the Soumu quick-reference table

Assumptions commonly attached to this table:

- Salary income earner
- No mortgage deduction or other special deductions
- Social insurance deduction is estimated as 15% of salary income
- Children age 15 or younger do not affect this table
- "Dual income" means the donor does not receive spouse special deduction
- "Spouse deduction" means the spouse has no income for this quick estimate

- `single_or_dual_income`
- `spouse_deduction`
